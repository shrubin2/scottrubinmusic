Dances and Meditations

2-channel acousmatic

Dances and Meditations was recorded, assembled, mixed and mastered by Scott Rubin in late 2020 in Chicago. This solo project explores the viola as an exclusive and versatile sound source; chopped, processed, and manipulated into 8 tracks of precarious characters.
